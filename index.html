About GeoTrivia

GeoTrivia is an educational app designed to help kids explore the world through fun quizzes and games. With GeoTrivia, children can:

  • Test their knowledge with quizzes on geography, history, and culture.
  • Challenge themselves with quizzes at different difficulty levels.
  • Learn new facts and expand their understanding of the world.
  • Track their progress and earn achievements as they play.

Our app combines learning with entertainment, making it a great tool for kids to enhance their social science skills while having fun. GeoTrivia is safe, kid-friendly, and designed to ignite curiosity and exploration.
